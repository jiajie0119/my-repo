How Can We Help?

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I access the sentiment analysis dashboard?

You can access the dashboard by logging into your account and navigating to the 'Home' tab. From there, you can insert your link for sentiment analysis.

2. How does the system analyze reviews?

Our system uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to classify user reviews into positive, neutral, or negative sentiments.

3. Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is securely stored. We follow standard practices to ensure data privacy and security.

4. Can I export the analysis results?

Yes, you can export the sentiment analysis results in PDF format directly from the dashboard.

5. Is the sentiment analysis in real-time?

Yes, once the system scrapes the reviews, the sentiment analysis is processed in real-time and displayed on the dashboard.

6. How often is the data updated?

The data is updated in real-time as soon as new reviews are scraped from the integrated platforms.
